# Fees

- Monthly account fee: $5 for Checking, waived if your balance stays above $1,500.
- ATM withdrawals are free at Northwind ATMs; $2.50 applies at other banks' ATMs.
- Overdraft fee: $15 per transaction, capped at one charge per day.
- International transfers cost $20 flat plus the exchange rate margin.
- There is no fee for online transfers between Northwind accounts.
