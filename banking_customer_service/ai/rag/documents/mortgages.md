# Mortgages

- Northwind Bank offers fixed-rate and variable-rate mortgages, with terms of 15, 20, and 30 years.
- The minimum down payment is 5% for first-time buyers and 10% for all other applicants.
- Pre-approval is free and valid for 90 days.
- You can make extra repayments at any time with no penalty on variable-rate mortgages.
- To apply, you need proof of income, two months of bank statements, and a valid government ID.
