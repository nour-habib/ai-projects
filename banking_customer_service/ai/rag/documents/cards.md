# Cards

- Debit cards are issued free with every Checking account and arrive within 5 to 7 business days.
- Credit cards offer 1.5% cashback on all purchases with no annual fee.
- You can freeze or unfreeze a lost card instantly in the mobile app.
- Replacement cards cost $10 and are delivered within 7 business days.
- Contactless payment and mobile wallet (Apple Pay, Google Pay) are supported on all cards.
