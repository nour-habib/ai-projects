# About Maple Family Health Clinic

## Overview

Maple Family Health Clinic is a community primary-care practice providing general
medical care for patients of all ages. Our team focuses on preventive care,
chronic disease management, and everyday health concerns in a welcoming setting.

## Opening hours

The clinic is open **Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM**. We are **closed on
weekends** (Saturday and Sunday) and on statutory holidays.

Appointment slots are **30 minutes** long. The last appointment of the day starts
at 4:30 PM.

## Holiday closures

The clinic is closed on all Ontario statutory holidays, including New Year's Day,
Family Day, Good Friday, Victoria Day, Canada Day, Labour Day, Thanksgiving,
Christmas Day, and Boxing Day. When a holiday falls on a weekday, the clinic
reopens the next business day.
