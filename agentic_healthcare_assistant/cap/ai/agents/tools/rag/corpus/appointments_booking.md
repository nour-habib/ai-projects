# Booking an Appointment

## How to book

Appointments can be booked through the clinic's online assistant, by phone at
(416) 555-0123, or in person at reception. Each appointment is **30 minutes**
long. Appointments are available Monday to Friday between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM.

When booking, please have ready the patient's name, date of birth, the reason for
the visit, and a preferred doctor or date if you have one.

## Choosing a doctor

The clinic has two general physicians, **Dr. Nguyen** and **Dr. Lee**. You may
request a specific doctor, or book with whichever physician has the earliest
available slot. New patients are welcome with either physician.

## Walk-ins

The clinic operates primarily by appointment. A limited number of same-day slots
are held for urgent concerns and released each morning at 9:00 AM — call ahead to
check availability. Walk-ins are accommodated when the schedule allows, but
booked appointments take priority.

## What to bring

Please bring your health card (OHIP) and any private insurance details. If you
are a new patient, arrive 10 minutes early to complete registration. Bring a list
of current medications and any relevant records from previous providers.

## Telephone and virtual visits

Some appointment types can be conducted by phone or video for established
patients. Let reception know when booking if you would prefer a virtual visit;
not all concerns are suitable for remote care.
