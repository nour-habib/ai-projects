# Services and Clinic Policies

## Services offered

Maple Family Health Clinic provides general primary care, including:

- Routine check-ups and physical exams
- Management of chronic conditions (e.g., diabetes, high blood pressure)
- Minor illness and injury care
- Vaccinations and immunizations
- Basic in-office procedures
- Referrals to specialists when needed

The clinic does **not** provide emergency care, surgery, or specialist services
on site. For emergencies, call 911.

## Prescriptions and refills

Refills are handled during appointments. For ongoing medications, your physician
may provide enough refills until your next visit. Refill requests outside of an
appointment can take up to **2 business days** to process — please plan ahead so
you do not run out.

## Referrals

If you need to see a specialist, your physician will arrange a referral. The
specialist's office will typically contact you directly to schedule. Referral
wait times depend on the specialty and are outside the clinic's control.

## Medical records and privacy

Your medical records are confidential and handled in accordance with Ontario's
Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA). You may request a copy or a
transfer of your records through reception; a records-transfer fee may apply.

## Languages

Staff at the clinic can assist patients in English and French. If you need an
interpreter for another language, let reception know when booking and we will do
our best to accommodate.
