# Location, Parking, and Getting Here

## Address

Maple Family Health Clinic is located at **123 Maple Avenue, Suite 200, Toronto,
ON M5V 2T6**. We are on the second floor of the Maple Medical Building; take the
elevator or stairs to Suite 200. Reception is directly to your right as you exit
the elevator.

## Parking

On-site parking is available in the building's underground garage, accessible
from Maple Avenue. Parking is **$3 per half hour, to a daily maximum of $18**.
Patients with an appointment can have their parking ticket validated at reception
for a **reduced flat rate of $5 for the day**.

Limited free street parking is available on side streets nearby, with a two-hour
maximum during business hours. There are two designated **accessible parking
spaces** near the garage elevator.

## Public transit

The clinic is a 5-minute walk from Maple Avenue Station. Bus routes 12, 45, and
501 stop within one block of the building. Bicycle racks are available at the
front entrance.

## Accessibility

The building and clinic are wheelchair accessible, with step-free entry, an
elevator, and an accessible washroom on the second floor. If you need any
accommodation for your visit, please let reception know in advance.
