# Insurance and Billing

## OHIP-covered visits

Most medically necessary visits are covered by the **Ontario Health Insurance
Plan (OHIP)**. Please bring a valid health card to every appointment. If your
health card is expired or you cannot present it, you may be billed directly for
the visit and asked to seek reimbursement.

## Private insurance

Some services and products (for example, certain vaccines, travel medicine, or
medical equipment) may be covered by private or workplace insurance plans. The
clinic provides itemized receipts you can submit to your insurer; we do not bill
private insurers directly.

## Uninsured services and fees

Some services are not covered by OHIP and are billed to the patient. Common
examples and typical fees:

- Sick notes / work or school notes: $20
- Form completion (insurance, disability, travel): $30–$75 depending on length
- Missed-appointment fee: $50
- Transfer of medical records: $30

A full fee schedule is available at reception. Fees are payable at the time of
service.

## Payment methods

The clinic accepts debit, Visa, Mastercard, and cash for uninsured services and
fees. Receipts are issued for all payments.
