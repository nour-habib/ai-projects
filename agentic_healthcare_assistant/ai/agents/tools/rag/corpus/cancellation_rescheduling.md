# Cancellation and Rescheduling

## Cancellation policy

If you need to cancel, please give at least **24 hours' notice** so the slot can
be offered to another patient. You can cancel online, by phone, or in person.

## Late cancellations and no-shows

Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice, or missed without notice
(a "no-show"), may be subject to a **$50 missed-appointment fee**. This fee is not
covered by OHIP. Repeated no-shows may affect your ability to pre-book future
appointments.

## Rescheduling

To reschedule, cancel your existing appointment and book a new time. There is no
charge to reschedule when done with at least 24 hours' notice. The online
assistant can cancel an existing appointment and help you find a new slot.

## Late arrival

If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, the clinic may need to shorten your
visit or rebook you for another day, depending on the schedule. Please call ahead
if you are running late.
